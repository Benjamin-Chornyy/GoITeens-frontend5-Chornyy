This is definitely the best project in the history of Minecraft servers. Here you can see a variety of mini-games, a great development team, great friendly players and a whole world of adventure. You have probably heard about this project many times.

Up to 50,000 players are online every day on the MineCreeperUK! In total, tens or even hundreds of millions of users are registered on the project. Can you imagine the scope of the project? You will always find here someone to chat with - be it foreign players or our compatriots.

Also on MineCreeperUK you will find chic maps and magnificent buildings. If you want to enjoy the beauty of the cubic world, you need to do it here. The project employs a specialized team of designers and architects who put their souls into each cube, carefully selecting the colors, shape and filling of buildings.
